Overiew of The Project

The 2123 Project is a visionary initiative celebrating Trinity College’s Bicentennial by imagining the world 100 years from now. Using AI-generated trading cards, historical archives, and community-driven ideas, we explore the innovations, culture, and possibilities of the future. Join us in envisioning Trinity’s Tercentennial by contributing your own futuristic vision!

Trinitys Vision

Trinity College aims to prepare students to be bold, independent thinkers, enable them to learn what they love, build their confidence, and become leaders and innovators, while supporting all members of the community in achieving their potential.

How It Works

  • Step 1: "Submit Your Vision" – Form where users share futuristic ideas.
  • Step 2: "AI Trading Cards" – AI generates illustrated concept cards.
  • Step 3: "Showcased in the Exhibit" – Selected ideas featured in a virtual & physical gallery.
